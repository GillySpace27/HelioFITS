# HelioFITS Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 14 July 2026_

HelioFITS does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- **No data collection.** The app gathers no information about you or your use of it.
- **No network access.** HelioFITS has no network entitlement — the macOS App Sandbox denies it all network access, so it cannot connect to the internet and nothing you open can leave your Mac.
- **No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no advertising.**
- **Local files only.** The app reads the FITS files you explicitly open, and writes PNG files only where you ask it to. It runs in the macOS App Sandbox and cannot access files you have not opened.

The only data HelioFITS stores are your own display preferences (such as which image extension to show for a folder), kept locally on your Mac in standard system storage. These never leave your device.
